Mighty Mouse: Wolf! Wolf!

Mighty Mouse: Wolf! Wolf! is a classic animated short film that was released in 1944. The film follows the adventures of Mighty Mouse, a superhero mouse who uses his superpowers to protect the citizens of Mouseville from danger.

In this particular film, Mighty Mouse must rescue a group of sheep from a wolf who is pretending to be Little Bo Peep in order to trick them. The film is known for its catchy theme song and fast-paced action. It is a fun and entertaining watch for fans of classic animation and superhero stories.
